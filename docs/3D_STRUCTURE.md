## ğŸ“ Material Specifications for 3D Structure

### Chassis Material Options

**Option 1: Acrylic Sheets**
- Thickness: 3mm for panels, 5mm for structural
- Transparent or colored
- Easy to cut with laser cutter
- Lightweight and rigid

**Option 2: Aluminum Frame**
- T-slot aluminum extrusions (20x20mm)
- More durable and professional
- Better heat dissipation
- Heavier but more stable

**Option 3: 3D Printed Parts**
- PLA or PETG filament
- Custom designed brackets
- Perfect fit for components
- Requires 3D printer access

## ğŸ”§ 3D Assembly Tips

1. **Build from bottom up**: Start with base, then torso, arms, head
2. **Test fit before securing**: Dry fit all components first
3. **Cable management**: Plan wire routing before final assembly
4. **Weight distribution**: Keep heavy components (batteries, Pi) low and centered
5. **Access panels**: Leave openings for maintenance
6. **Ventilation**: Ensure airflow for electronics cooling
