## üìù Notes

1. **Quality Matters**: For servos and power supplies, invest in quality brands to avoid failures
2. **Buy Extra**: Get 10-20% extra of small components (wires, resistors, etc.)
3. **Check Compatibility**: Ensure all components work with 5V logic levels
4. **Power Planning**: Calculate total power consumption before finalizing power supply
5. **Start Small**: You can build a basic version first and add features incrementally
