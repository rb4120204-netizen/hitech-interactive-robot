## üí° Cost-Saving Tips for India

### 1. Buy Local When Possible
- Servo motors: ‚Çπ500-600 each locally vs ‚Çπ650 online
- Acrylic sheets: 30-40% cheaper from local vendors
- Screws & hardware: Much cheaper at local hardware stores

### 2. Bundle Purchases
- Buy all electronics from one seller for shipping discounts
- Group orders with friends for bulk discounts

### 3. Alternative Components
- **Instead of Raspberry Pi 4 (‚Çπ6,200):**
  - Raspberry Pi 3B+ (‚Çπ3,500-4,000) - Still works well
  - Orange Pi (‚Çπ2,500-3,500) - Budget alternative

- **Instead of branded servos:**
  - Generic MG996R clones (‚Çπ400-500 each)
  - Still reliable for hobby projects

### 4. Reuse & Recycle
- Old phone chargers for power supply
- Salvage USB cables from old devices
- Reuse cardboard/wood for initial prototyping
